The Shift

Software Changed. The Workflow Didn't.

Traditional software is predictable. AI-powered software isn't. That demands a fundamentally different approach.

Traditional Software
AI-Powered Software
How it works Same input = same output, every time Same input can produce different results each time
Where effort goes 80–90% building: writing logic, fixing bugs 80–90% evaluating: checking outputs, tuning quality
When is it "done"? When the bugs are fixed, you ship it Never — continuous monitoring and improving
When things go wrong Bugs are predictable and reproducible Failures are subtle, inconsistent, and can drift
The human's role Operates the system: fills forms, clicks buttons Supervises the system: reviews output, approves or rejects

Businesses need guarantees — precise compliance, exact calculations, auditable decisions. We're building the architecture that delivers those guarantees using technology that, by its nature, doesn't offer them.

How It Works

Three Steps. One Human Checkpoint.

Instead of a black box, the AI shows its work at every stage — and waits for approval before acting.

1
Extract

"Here's what I found"

The AI reads your document and pulls out structured data — rules, thresholds, dates, organizations — mapped to your database fields.

Human Checkpoint
2
Plan

"Here's what I'll do"

Before anything touches the database, the AI shows its plan in plain language. You say "yes" or "no, fix this."

3
Execute

"Approved? Done."

Once you sign off, the system follows the approved plan exactly. No AI judgment. Deterministic. Auditable.

Over Time

Trust Is Earned, Not Declared

Every approval and rejection trains the system. Over time, routine work flows through automatically.

Phase 1

Hands-On

Review every output. Rate each step. The system builds a reliability baseline.

Phase 2

Building Trust

Trust high-confidence results. Focus your attention on what the system flags as uncertain.

Phase 3

Autonomous

Routine documents flow through automatically. You handle exceptions only.

The Platform

More Than a Policy Analyzer

FPI is a complete attendance management platform — the AI pipeline sits on top of a production-grade compliance system.

Policy Upload

PDF, DOCX, or paste text. AI extracts rules and generates an ingestion plan.

Points-Based Discipline

Configurable rules, thresholds, and rolling windows. Automatic scoring on clock-in records.

Multi-Tenant

Regions, organizations, and policies scoped per entity. Labor law context per region.

Audit Trail

Every point change — violation or manual override — is recorded and traceable.

Alerts & Red Flags

Threshold-based alerts when employees approach disciplinary action.

Playground Mode

Safe sandbox with sample policies and seed data. Experiment without production risk.
